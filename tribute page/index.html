Kurt Donald Cobain

Nirvana vocalist

Here's a time line of Kurt Cobain life

  • Feb 20, 1967 Kurt Cobain was born
  • Jun 16, 1981 Formed his first band
  • Oct 7, 1985 gave his first concerts
  • Mar 23, 1988 He recorded his first album
  • Jan 4, 1990 released his second album: Nevermind
  • Jan 1, 1992 his third album came out: Incesticide
  • Feb 24, 1992 Kurt married Courtney Love
  • Jan 1, 1993 is hospitalized for overdose
  • Apr 5, 1994 dies from a gunshot to the head
  • Jan 1, 1995 an album was published in his honor

Cobain's best known song is the grunge anthem, "Smells like teen spirit." Behind that song is a story: Kathleen Hanna, a friend of Cobain, wrote on the wall of her room "Kurt smells like Teen Spirit", alluding to the Teen Spirit deodorant worn by Kurt's girlfriend at the time, Tobi Vail. Kurt did not understand the joke and interpreted the phrase with a revolutionary meaning.
